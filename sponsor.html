Kanishk's FAQs

Who is Swapnil Mathur sir?

Swapnil Mathur sir is a highly experienced and renowned educator with over 23 years of teaching expertise. He specializes in coaching students for the MPPSC (Madhya Pradesh Public Service Commission) exam and has helped numerous students achieve their goals of qualifying for this prestigious examination.

What is MPPSC, and why is it important?

MPPSC stands for Madhya Pradesh Public Service Commission. It is a state-level civil services examination conducted by the government of Madhya Pradesh. Qualifying the MPPSC exam opens up opportunities to serve in various administrative roles within the state, making it a significant milestone for aspirants seeking a career in civil services.

What courses are offered at Swapnil Mathur's MPPSC Coaching Centre?

Our coaching centre provides comprehensive courses designed specifically for MPPSC aspirants. These courses cover all the relevant subjects, including General Studies, History, Geography, Polity, Economy, Environment, and more, to ensure students are well-prepared for all stages of the MPPSC exam.

How is the coaching at Swapnil Mathur's different from others?

At Swapnil Mathur's coaching centre, the teaching methodology is unparalleled. Swapnil sir's 23 years of experience, expertise in the MPPSC syllabus, and personalized attention to each student make the coaching exceptional. The focus on critical thinking, practical learning, and regular assessments sets us apart from other coaching centres.

Is there any demo class available to experience the coaching?

Yes, we provide demo classes to give students an opportunity to experience the teaching style and approach of Swapnil Mathur sir. The demo classes offer insights into our coaching methodology and allow students to assess the quality of our courses before making a commitment.

What study materials are provided to the students?

Our coaching centre provides well-researched and comprehensive study materials that cover the entire MPPSC syllabus. These materials are regularly updated to ensure they align with the latest exam pattern and changes in the curriculum.

Are there doubt-clearing sessions for students?

Yes, we understand the importance of clarifying doubts to enhance the learning process. We conduct regular doubt-clearing sessions where students can ask questions and get them resolved by Swapnil Mathur sir and our experienced faculty.
